Trip Details

Viewing Detailed Information

  1. 1.

    On the Trip Detail page, you will find additional information about the selected trip.

    The page may include details such as:

    • Trip Name
    • Location
    • Accommodation Information
    • Map with Location of the trip
  2. 2.

    You can browse accomodation images, by clicking forward or backwards arrow buttons under accomodation image!

  3. 3.

    Select check in date from the date picked on the right.Note that the check in date has to be at least one day from today!

    Select check out date from the date picked on the right.Note that the check out date has to be at least one day from check in date!

  4. 4.

    Click on reserve button to make a reservation

    If your input were valid, you will be redirected to a page where you can add attractions and restaurant for your trip
