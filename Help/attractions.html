Attractions UserControl

Adding Attractions

  1. 1.

    The Page consists of two main sections:

    • The left section displays a list of attractions and restaurants.
    • The right section contains a map where attractions and restaurants are plotted.
  2. 2.

    Attractions and restaurants are shown based on the city of the trip's accommodation.

  3. 3.

    Users can interact with attractions and restaurants in the following ways:

    • To add an attraction or restaurant to the trip, click on the corresponding marker
    • If the attraction or restaurant is not already included in the trip, it will be added.
    • If the attraction or restaurant is already included in the trip, it will be removed.
  4. 4.

    The total price of the trip is displayed at the bottom of the Page.

    Users can place an order by clicking the "Order" button.

  5. 5.

    If the order is successful you will be notified about it.
