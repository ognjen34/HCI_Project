Login Form

Logging In

  1. 1.

    Open the login form by launching the application.

    On the login form, you will see two fields: Username and Password.

  2. 2.

    Enter your username in the Username field.

    Enter your password in the Password field. Note that the password characters are masked for security.

    Click the Login button to proceed with the login.

  3. 3.

    If your login credentials are correct, you will be successfully logged in to the application.

    If your login credentials are incorrect, an error message will be displayed indicating that the login information is incorrect. Please review your username and password and try again.

Registering

  1. 1.

    If you don't have an account and need to register, click the Register button on the login form.

  2. 2.

    This will navigate you to the registration page where you can create a new account.

    Once registered, you can return to the login form and use your newly created credentials to log in.
