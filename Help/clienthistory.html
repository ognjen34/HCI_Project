History

Viewing Order History

  1. 1.

    On the History page, you will see a list of your ordered trips.

    The history items on the page will display the following information:

    • Trip Name
    • Location
    • Check-in Date
    • Check-out Date
    • Total Price
    • Status
  2. 2.

    To view detailed information about a trip, click on the Details button associated with the trip.

    This will open a page with more information about the trip.
