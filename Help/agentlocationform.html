Form for Adding/Updating Locations

  1. 1.

    Fill out the basic informations of the form.

  2. 2.

    Enter location address in the Address field.

    Enter location city in the City field.

  3. 3.

    You can also select location, by double-clicking on the map.Note that the clicked location has to be in Serbia!.

  4. 4.

    You can check if you entered your desired location, by clicking Show on Map.

  5. 5.

    To finish adding/updationg location, click on the save button, or if you changed your mind you can click on the back button
