History Detail

Viewing Detailed Information

  1. 1.

    On the History Detail page, you will find additional information about the selected trip.

    The page may include details such as:

    • Trip Name
    • Location
    • Check-in Date
    • Check-out Date
    • Total Price
    • Accommodation Information
    • List of Restaurants
    • List of Attractions
    • Map with Pinned Attractions and Restaurants
  2. 2.

    To zoom in on an attraction on the map, simply click on the attraction or restaurant from the list on the right. This will zoom the map to focus on the selected attraction.

    If you want to zoom out to the accommodation location, click on the location text under the map. This will zoom the map out to show the entire accommodation area.
